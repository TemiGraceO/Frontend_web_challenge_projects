About Me

I’m Micheal Chibueze Achilihu, a Global Aftersales Manager at Adtec Healthcare, London, leading global service strategy for medical devices using cold atmospheric plasma to improve wound care outcomes.

With a foundation in Biomedical and Electrical Engineering, I’ve evolved from a hands-on field engineer to a global leader overseeing service engineering, customer support models, and Design-for-Serviceability (DFS) integration.

My leadership combines innovation, system excellence, and integrity — bridging engineering precision and customer-focused service that drives impact across global healthcare systems.
